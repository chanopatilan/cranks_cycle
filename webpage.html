[About Us]

Welcome to Cranks Cycle!

-At Cranks Cycle,we’re driven by a passion for cycling and a commitment to excellence. Founded in 2024, our mission is to bring from budget meals to high-quality bicycles, accessories, and expert repair services to cyclists of all levels. Whether you’re a seasoned rider, a weekend warrior, or just starting your cycling journey, we’re here to ensure you have the best experience on two wheels.

Our Vision

-We believe in the power of cycling to transform lives and communities. Our vision is to inspire more people to embrace cycling as a sustainable, healthy, and joyful way of life. At Cranks Cycle, every customer is part of our cycling family, and we’re dedicated to helping you ride further, faster, and with confidence.

Join the Journey

-Whether you’re shopping for a new bike, need expert advice, or want to keep your bike in top shape, Cranks Cycle is here for you. Let’s ride together and make every journey an adventure.

[Bike Shop Services]

Bike Repair & Maintenance:

*Tune-ups (basic, advanced, or full tune-up packages)
*Flat tire repair (patching or tube replacement)
*Brake adjustments (hydraulic and mechanical brakes)
*Gear shifting tune-ups (derailleur adjustments)
*Wheel truing (fixing wobbly wheels)
*Chain cleaning and lubrication
*Suspension servicing (forks and shocks)
*Hydraulic brake bleeding

Bike Customization:

*Custom build services (from frame to finishing)
*Upgrading components (gears, wheels, handlebars, etc.)
*Personalized fitting (ensuring the bike fits you perfectly)

Bike Rentals:

*Hourly, daily, or weekly bike rentals (city bikes, mountain bikes, road bikes)
*E-bike rentals for easy city commuting or long-distance rides

Bike Fitting:

*Professional bike fitting to optimize comfort and performance
*Saddle fitting (with a variety of saddles to try)
*Pedal and cleat alignment for better cycling efficiency

Group Rides and Events:

*Organized group rides for various skill levels (casual, competitive, endurance)
*Cycling workshops (maintenance, repair, safety)
*Bike safety and riding technique courses
